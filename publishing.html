TOKIWA PUBLISHING

In Tokiwa Publishing Haus, our aim is to develop and publish relevant education resource materials to support teaching and learning in the classroom. We have published English readers – Big books, story books, poem books; factual information readers and posters suitable for prep to Grade 8. We have in development, books addressing national social issues especially ‘gender-based violence’.

We publish a range of quality and affordable support materials for schools to implement the national curriculum. Papua New Guineans have written many of our books.
