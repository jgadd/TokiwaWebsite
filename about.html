Publishing

In Tokiwa Publishing Haus, our aim is to develop and publish relevant education resource materials to support teaching and learning in the classroom.


We have published English readers – Big books, story books, poem books; factual information readers and posters suitable for prep to Grade 8.

We have in development, books addressing national social issues especially ‘gender-based violence’.

Education & Materials

We have successfully executed government tenders – print and distributed basic school materials to schools.

We procure and supply textbooks for the Department of Education and distribute these materials to schools throughout the country.

We can assist schools by sourcing materials for their specific needs.
